HTML Lists

HTML lists are defined with the <ul> and <ol> tags.

Example:

        
            <ul>
                <li>Item 1</li>
                <li>Item 2</li>
            </ul>
            <ol>
                <li>First step</li>
                <li>Second step</li>
            </ol>

Rendered Output:

  • Item 1
  • Item 2
  1. First step
  2. Second step
